ABOUT US

Vichar Club is one of the clubs of University College of Engineering And Technology which was created on 24th July,2020.It focuses on inculcating the feeling of competitiveness among the students to enhance their skills and to let them open up to new technologies and new ideas.


"Vichar" meaning "idea" as the name suggests has a motive "To make students think, generate an idea, hypothesize and help them to take it in the form of prototype."


This club organises various contests like technical quizes, essay writing, coding competitiom, web designing and many more. It helps the students to look into the world outside their campus arena as students from all over takes part in the competition.It is a deliberate attempt to bridge the gap between the technicalities among the students across the nation.


So keep sharing your "Vichar".
Remember never let your ideas die.
